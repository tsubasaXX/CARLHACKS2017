Become a mentor

Think back to when you were young. Do you remember having a hard time making friends or your parents got a divorce? Did you move to a new city in 5th grade?

Mentoring helps because it guarantees a young person that there is someone who cares about them. A child is not alone in dealing with day-to-day challenges. Mentors provide their mentees with an experienced friend who is there to help in any number of situations.

Project Friendship has been pairing St. Olaf and Carleton students with little buddies for 49 years! I welcome you to join us, to be a mentor for a child who needs a positive experience and to help them build their self esteem! To see that child grow into a teenager that is on the right path to success!
